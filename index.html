Women In Engineering Affinity Group - Yarmouk University Branch

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is a global network of IEEE members and volunteers dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests in a career in engineering and science.

About Us

We are a non-profit affinity group, affiliated with the IEEE Jordan Section and based in Yarmouk University, Irbid. We are focused on women in STEM, supporting them as much as possible and voluntary work!

Team

A group of passionate, youthful women that lovingly gathered to help pave the road to a brighter future for females in STEM, each with her own unique set of skills. We work hand in hand to reach the top as we believe that teamwork is fundamental for a better life.

Vision

Our absolute faith in women! We seek female empowerment and awareness for all women and we aim to provide them with the right resources to put them out in the world as strong independent women capable of insuing change and infuencing the course of life.

History

Launched in 2019 in Yarmouk University, we began our journey towards the top! Gaining traction over the years, we became the largest affinity group among our peers in Jordan, growing our family of members constantly.
