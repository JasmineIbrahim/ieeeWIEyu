Our Events

Ranging from voluntary work to building technical skills, we provide and host all sorts of beneficial and recreational events for our members and with their help. We emphasize on the role of women in society, be it scientific or social, and we shed light on that through the activities we host! Remember, we are here to build a network, keep it growing and keep on pushing forth, and what way to do that than allow women in our community to partake in the organization and attendance of events and social gatherings! We plant the seeds of hard and soft skills and walk our members and volunteers through the steps of making those seeds grow.
